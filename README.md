## She Wires Words

She Wires Words is a 501(c)(3) status organization that is fiscally sponsored by Hack Club. She Wires Words aims to empower females in tech fields and provide access to classes, materials, and education in tech to students around the world. 

### Our Website
- This code encompasses our website, which has been used in 40+ countries worldwide for learning
- It contains study guides, tutorials, and materials to help students learn about tech.
